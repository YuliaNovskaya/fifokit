Common questions

FIFO questions answered clearly.

Can I get FIFO work with no mining experience?

Yes, but the best pathway depends on your background, tickets, location, fitness, and willingness to start in entry-level roles.

Which tickets should I get first?

Start with tickets that match your target role. The roadmap helps you avoid spending money on tickets that are not useful yet.

Is this focused on Western Australia?

Yes. FIFOKIT is built around WA FIFO pathways, employers, rosters, and entry-level mining job preparation.

Is this a course?

No. It is a practical starter toolkit with PDFs, templates, job links, calculator tools, and short starter videos.
