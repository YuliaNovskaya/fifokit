Overview

FIFOKIT respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect personal information when you use fifokit.com, purchase digital products, download resources or sign up for updates.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, email address, purchase details, form submissions, website usage data and any information you choose to provide when contacting us.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to deliver digital products, send requested downloads, respond to enquiries, improve FIFOKIT, manage customer records, process orders and send updates you have requested.

Third-Party Services

FIFOKIT may use third-party services including Stripe, Airtable, Make, Google Drive, GitHub Pages, email providers and analytics tools to operate the website and deliver products.

Email Communications

If you sign up for updates or request a download, we may email you about FIFOKIT resources, job updates, tools and related products. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss, unauthorised access, modification or disclosure.

Access and Correction

You may request access to your personal information or ask us to correct it by contacting us at ops@fifokit.com .

Questions? Contact Us

For privacy questions, contact us at ops@fifokit.com .
