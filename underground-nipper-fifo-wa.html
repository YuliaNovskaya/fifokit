FIFO Underground Nipper Jobs in WA

FIFO Underground Nipper roles are one of the most common entry points into mining support services in Western Australia.

What the role involves

Underground Nippers usually support mine site camps with cleaning, kitchen hand duties, housekeeping, laundry, dining room service and general site support.

Common requirements

  • Driver Licence
  • National Police Check
  • Ability to pass medical and drug/alcohol screening
  • Hospitality, cleaning or manual work experience can help

Typical rosters

Common rosters include 2:1, 1:1, 8:6 and other FIFO arrangements depending on employer and site.

Beginner-friendly rating

High. This is one of the better FIFO options for people trying to enter the mining industry without previous mine site experience.
